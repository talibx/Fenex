1. System Overview

This is a Laravel-based local inventory and product management website that I use to track my stock and shipments to Amazon.ae.

The website keeps records of products and inventories.

When I ship products to Amazon, I update the quantity here to know how many pieces remain locally.

When I receive new stock from China or local suppliers, I first add it here before sending part of it to Amazon.

This system helps me manage local stock levels and track what has been shipped or kept for local sales.

2. Current Main Pages

Products Page

Shows all products I own.

Displays product details (SKU, name, cost, etc.).

Inventory Page

Shows how many pieces I have in stock.

Allows adding or subtracting quantities (e.g., new stock arrivals, shipments to Amazon).

When I add inventory, it shows a list of products so I can update their quantities.
